Welcome, Students!

At Kongu Engineering College, we believe in building a community where students and alumni can interact, collaborate, and grow together. Our Alumni-Student Platform is designed to help students connect with alumni for mentorship, career guidance, internships, and job opportunities. By registering, you will gain access to valuable insights from our esteemed alumni and participate in a variety of professional development programs.
